Corpus developement

This chapter discusses the techniques used to design a good corpus for recording for use in general speech synthesis. The basic requirements for a speech synthesis corpus are: phonetically prosodically balanced targeted toward the intended domain(s) easy to say by voice talent without mistakes short enough for the voice talent to be willing to say it.
